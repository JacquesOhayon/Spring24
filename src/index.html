What is the Antibiotic Reduction Project?

We are the Antibiotic Reduction Project, or ARP and we seek to bring attention to a very serious problem of antibiotic resistance. This occurs when bacteria change genetically in ways that makes current drugs or methods ineffective. The process also allows for more resistant strains of bacteria to proliferate and weaken our immune system.

Our Mission

We believe more regulated presrcriptions in antibiotics will reduce the number of people susceptible to fungal outbreaks. With healthy eating, proper rest, and hygiene; your own microbiota will be boosted. What is Vapor Pressure Defecit(VPD)?

Vapor Pressure Deficit is the difference between the amount of moisture in the air and how much moisture the air can hold when it is saturated.
